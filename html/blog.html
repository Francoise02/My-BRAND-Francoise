Microsoft Announces The Game Of Learners Season 3 Winners


A Microsoft Africa Development Center (ADC) initiative, the Game of Learners (GOL) engages directly with students regardless of the higher education institution they come from. Born virtual, GOL is a program without borders. It provides a virtual environment where students learn computer science technologies and practices hands-on while they build impactful solutions for their community.



African Quartet Wins Global Technology Prize Beating 10,000 Other Contestants


Four Rwandan students enrolled at the African Leadership University beat 10,000 other Rising Leaders to emerge winners at the first-ever global Make Reality Challenge hosted by the non-profit professional association, Project Management Institute (PMI). The contestants had to bring creative and strategic solutions to life using no-code/low-code tools while making a social impact.



Breaking new ground: The Queen's Gambit Rwanda Third Cycle


The Queen’s Gambit is a unique, girl-friendly 12-week educational project focusing on gender knowledge and skills development for secondary school girls in Rwanda. It also involves the study and practice of the game of chess as a learning tool to foster numerous developmental benefits; advanced critical thinking and mathematics skills, pattern recognition and analysis, and problem solving
